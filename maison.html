Our Journey

From Struggle to Success

The full story of how Maison was built — the hard way.

🏠 2008 — Born in a Lisbon Apartment

Maison began on a kitchen table in a cramped Lisbon apartment with one ageing laptop, €18,000 in personal savings, and one stubborn belief: that people everywhere deserve access to the same quality. Two best friends — one with a background in logistics, one in design — burned through their savings and were rejected by 14 investors in the first year alone. They coded the website themselves. They slept in shifts.

🔝 2010–2012 — The Grinding Years

Three years of operating at a loss. Orders were packed by hand from a storage unit. There were months without salaries. Suppliers cancelled contracts. One co-founder nearly walked away. What kept them going were the customers — a small but fiercely loyal group who told their friends, who told their friends. Their first sales month brought in €340. They framed the screenshot.

🌟 2013 — The Article that Changed Everything

A feature in a respected European lifestyle magazine described Maison as "the quiet revolution in curated retail." Within 72 hours the site crashed three times. 18,000 new signups in a week. A Paris-based impact fund reached out days later and offered €1.2M in seed funding. They negotiated to €1.8M and hired their first 12 employees.

🌎 2015–2018 — Building Something Real

A proper warehouse. A customer service team. Brand partnerships with Toteme, The Row, and Aesop — each won through in-person meetings, often funded out of pocket. The London office opened in 2017. Fashion, electronics, and farm-fresh produce all live under one roof. The team grew to 85 people across three countries.

💦 2020 — When COVID Almost Ended It

Revenue fell 58% in eight weeks. International shipping collapsed. Three key brand partners suspended operations. Instead of cutting staff, Maison pivoted: a farm-direct model connecting West African farmers directly to European consumers. It saved 240 farming families and tripled farm revenue by year end. "The crisis forced us to find our soul," the founders said.

🏠🚗 2022–2024 — Houses, Cars and Ambition

Expansion into property and automotive was a natural step. The same customers who trusted Maison for a coat trusted them for a car or a home. Maison Estates listed its 1,000th property. Maison Motors verified its 500th vehicle. A Series B of €28M followed, led by a consortium of European and African impact investors. 500,000 customers across 12 countries.

Today. Still just beginning.

"We started with the belief that people everywhere deserve the same quality. That belief has never changed. We are not finished. We are just beginning."
— The Maison Founders

Our Story

Fashion, Home &
Everything In Between

Founded 2018

Built on the belief that quality shouldn't be a luxury

Maison was founded in a small Lisbon apartment by two friends who were tired of choosing between beautiful things and responsible choices. We believed then — as we do now — that great design, ethical production, and fair pricing are not mutually exclusive.

Today we work with over 500 brands, makers, and independent designers across 40 countries. Every single product on Maison is reviewed by our team before it goes live. We've rejected more than we've accepted — and we're proud of that.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Quality First
Every product is reviewed before listing. We prioritise longevity over trend-chasing — things made to last, not to be thrown away after one season.
Sustainability
We partner only with brands that meet our environmental standards. GOTS, OEKO-TEX, Fair Wear — certifications matter to us and to our planet.
Community
From the artisans we work with to the customers who shop with us — Maison is built on relationships, not transactions.
Transparency
We tell you where things are made, what they're made of, and why they cost what they do. Honest pricing. No mystery markups.

The Story of Maison

From Nothing,
to Everything.

2008
The Struggle Begins
Maison was born in a small apartment in Douala, Cameroon. Founder Elise Mbarga had one laptop, no funding, and a vision: to bring luxury goods to Africa at fair prices. She coded the first version herself, rejected by 14 investors.
2011
First 100 Customers
After three years of grinding, Maison reached 100 loyal customers. Elise hand-packed every order from her living room. A viral article in a French fashion magazine changed everything.
2014
First Funding Round
A Paris-based impact fund invested 1.2M EUR, seeing the vision. Maison opened its first warehouse in Yaounde and hired 12 people. Fashion, electronics, and farm produce all launched the same year.
2017
Expansion to Europe
The London office opened. Maison became the first African luxury marketplace with a physical presence in Europe. Partnership with Toteme, The Row, and Aesop followed.
2020
Pandemic Pivot
COVID-19 nearly broke the company. But Elise pivoted to a farm-direct model, connecting Cameroonian farmers to consumers across Europe. This saved 240 farms and tripled Maison farm revenue.
2022
Houses and Cars Launch
Expanding beyond products into property and automotive. Maison listed its 1,000th property and 500th vehicle. The brand became the go-to platform for the African diaspora seeking luxury goods internationally.
2025
The Future is Now
500,000 customers. 12 countries. $280M in annual GMV. Elise was named to Forbes Africa 30 Under 30. Maison is preparing for Series C to expand into the Middle East and Southeast Asia.
A Message from our Founder

We started with nothing but a belief that people everywhere deserve access to the same quality. We are not finished. We are just beginning.

— Elise Mbarga, Founder & CEO

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device, remember your preferences, and improve your experience over time.

Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and pixel tags (also called web beacons). This policy covers all of these technologies collectively referred to as "cookies".

Some cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. Others are optional and are only placed on your device with your consent. You have full control over optional cookies — you can accept or reject them individually using our preference centre below.

Return window: 30 days from the date of delivery.

Condition: Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached and in original packaging. Items showing signs of wear, washing, alteration, or damage by the customer are not eligible for return.

How to return: Initiate a return through your Maison account or by emailing [email protected] with your order number and reason for return. We will provide a prepaid return label for defective items. For change-of-mind returns, the return shipping cost is borne by the customer.

Exchanges: Size and colour exchanges are accepted within the 30-day window subject to availability. Contact us first before returning to confirm availability.

Exceptions: Underwear, swimwear, pierced jewellery, and personalised/monogrammed items cannot be returned for hygiene and personalisation reasons unless defective.

3. Electronics & Appliances

Return window: 14 days from the date of delivery for change-of-mind returns. 12-month warranty coverage for manufacturing defects.

Damage: Physical damage caused by the customer (drops, liquid damage, unauthorised modifications) is not covered. Please inspect all items upon delivery and report damage within 48 hours.

Refund: Approved returns will be refunded within 5–10 business days to your original payment method after the item has been inspected by our technical team.

How to report: Take a clear photograph of the affected item and contact us via WhatsApp or email ([email protected]) with your order number. A credit or replacement will be arranged within 24 hours.

Change of mind: Due to food safety regulations, fresh produce orders cannot be returned once delivered. Change-of-mind cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before the scheduled delivery window.

5. Property Viewings

Viewing deposit refunds: Deposits paid to secure a property viewing are fully refundable if cancelled at least 48 hours before the scheduled viewing time.

Late cancellation: Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the viewing forfeit 50% of the deposit. This fee is passed to the property agent to compensate for their time.

No-show: Failure to attend a scheduled viewing without prior cancellation forfeits the full deposit.

Agent cancellation: If the agent or vendor cancels the viewing, your full deposit will be refunded within 3 business days with no deduction.

Refund method: Refunds are returned to the original payment method. Mobile Money refunds may take up to 5 business days depending on your network operator.

Late cancellation: Cancellations within 24 hours forfeit 25% of the deposit to cover the dealership's preparation costs.

Rental cancellations: Rentals cancelled 72+ hours before the rental start date receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72–24 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable.

Vehicle damage: Any damage to a vehicle during a test drive or rental period is the customer's responsibility. An excess/deductible may apply depending on the rental agreement signed with the operator.

Operator cancellation: If the dealer or rental operator cancels, a full refund is issued within 3 business days.

Step 1 — Sign in to your Maison account and navigate to Orders & Bookings.

Step 2 — Select the order or booking you wish to return or cancel and click "Request Refund / Return".

Step 3 — Provide details including your reason for return and any supporting photographs if applicable.

Step 4 — Await confirmation. Our team will review your request within 1–2 business days and send you a confirmation with next steps.

Step 5 — Return item (where applicable) using the prepaid label or instructions provided. Keep your proof of postage until the refund is confirmed.

Alternatively, you can email [email protected] with your order number and reason. We aim to respond to all refund requests within 24 hours.
